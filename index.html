Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Information about chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Shivaji maharaj was born at the shivneri fort near junnar in pune district on 19th feb 1630. shivaji's mother,Jijabai was the doughter of Lakhuji jadhavrao of sindkhed.His father Shahajiraje bhosale was a prominent sardar in the Deccan.At the time of shivaji maharaj's birth,most of the territory in maharashtra was under the possesion of the Nizamshaha of Ahmednagar and the Adilshaha of Bijapur. In the coastal belt of konkan, there were two sea powers, the portuguese and the Siddi. the British and Dutch who were engaged in expanding their trade also had their factories on the coast

Shivaji maharaj is one of the great national heroes of mahadeshwara. He created an independent and sovereign state in maharashtra that was based on justice , the welfare of the people, and tolerance to all faiths.The aims, objectives and Rajnitee of the maratha swaraj under Chhatrapati shivaji maharaj provided a new direction to the contemporary politics of india. In course of time ,his movement assumed, the form of an all-india struggle ; a struggle that was to change the political map of india.

several small spurs run eastward from the sahydri in pune region.the extremely rugged valleys enclosed by these are usually known as the mavals or khores each named after the stream running through it, or after the principle village.Collectively they are known as the maval.He skillfully utilized the geographical features of the maval region for the foundation of the swaraj.he created a feeling of trust and affection in minds of the people.many associates ,companions,and mavals joind him in his work of founding swaraj

The objective of shivaji maharaj in founding the swaraj is clearly expressed in his official seal or mudra which is in sanskrit.Through this mudra,Shivaji maharaj assured this people that the ever-incresing like the crescent moon, the kingdom of shivaji,son of shahaji,will always seek the welfare of the people.The Rajyabhishek ceremony (coronation)of shivaji maharaj was performed on June 6,1674 at Raigad . he assumed the title of a king in a formal coronation and become the first Chhatrapati of Maratha empire.Gaga bhatt the famous pandit of banaras, conducted the Rajyabhisheka of Shivaji maharaj.Chhatrapati shivaji maharaj died at 12 noon ,3rd,april,in 1680 at raigad , after running a fever for three weeks . it is said that he died due to contracting a disease bloody flux,,intestine anthrox. the funeral ceremony was arranged in raigad ,in presence of his son rajaram,and soyrabai. after shivaji maharaj death his elder son sambhaji and soyrabai,fought for control of the kingdom. after a brief struggle sambhaji maharaj was crowned king.
